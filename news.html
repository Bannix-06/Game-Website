Resident Evil: requiem

The main character, Grace Ashcroft, an FBI agent and Alyssa's daughter from the Resident Evil Outbreak game, travels to the devastated Raccoon City to investigate a series of mysterious deaths at the Renwood Hotel. She has to not only solve the mystery of the murders, but also understand how they relate to her past.

Release date: February 27, 2026

The sequel to Atomic Heart tells about new events unfolding in a retro futuristic universe with an alternate history. This time, the action is reaching global proportions.
